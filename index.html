CAMPUSCONNECT IS THE FUTURE OF CHAT

Designed by students for students

CampusConnect allows students to connect to other students all over the world whether it be for studying or hanging out.

Secure Private rooms

Campus connect creates unique rooms each having their own unique ID meaning that only people you give the room ID to can access the rooms. Perfect for private study groups.

No app required

Campus Connect can be accessed directly through your web browser, there's no need to bloat your mobile phone with apps!
